Detailed Description

template<typename BASE>
class WECore::LookAndFeelMixins::MidAnchoredRotarySlider< BASE >

Based on RotarySliderV2, but fills the right side of the slider when above half of the slider's value, and fills the left half when below.

Uses the following colours:

  1. ** Slider::rotarySliderFillColourId **
  2. ** Slider::rotarySliderOutlineColourId **
